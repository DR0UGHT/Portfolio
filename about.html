Passionate Game Developer

My name is Liam and I am a game developer and programmer with a passion for all things gaming. I am currently in Ottawa Ontario but grew up in Edmonton Alberta. Throughout my life I have been persuing game development wether it be through indie games or collaborating in school on small idea projects. As of July 2024, I will have my degree in Computer Science from Carleton University. I am a big fan of all types of games, but I love story games the most since so many aspects of the game can connect with a player. I have played games my whole life and have gotten enjoyment from every era the game industry had from space shooters like space invaders, to games that revolutionized FPS like half-life, all the way into modern day where the visuals of God of War paired with the story have changed the undustry in other ways. I am open to opportunities globally and am looking forward to new adventures in Game Development.
